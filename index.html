Hotel Policies

Parking

Complimentary self-parking is available for all guests. Valet parking services can be arranged upon request for an additional charge.

Pets

We are a pet-friendly hotel! Well-behaved pets are welcome with a small nightly fee. Please inquire about our pet policy for more details.

CEYLAN TREES is a non-smoking property. Smoking is strictly prohibited in all indoor areas, including guest rooms and common spaces. Designated outdoor smoking areas are available.
